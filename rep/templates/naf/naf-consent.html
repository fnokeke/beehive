Consent Form

Researchers can be contacted via Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Researchers’ Statement

We are asking you to participate in a research study. The purpose of this consent form is to give you the information you will need to help you decide whether to participate in the study or not. Please listen to what I am saying carefully. You may ask questions about the purpose of the research, what we ask you to do, the possible risks and benefits, your rights as a volunteer, and anything else about the research or this form that is not clear. When we have answered all your questions, you can decide if you want to be in the study or not. This process is called “informed consent.” We will repeat this information as many times you will like us to repeat.

PURPOSE OF THE STUDY

The purpose of this study is to understand your response to videos watched.

STUDY PROCEDURES

We will show you three videos one after the other. Each video is less than 5 minutes of length. Once you watch each video, you will report how you feel after watching the video and then provide feedback to the researcher based on verbal questions asked. You may quit the interview at any time for any reason. The time commitment for the study is around 30 minutes. During the course of interview, we will not ask you to enter any identifiable information about yourself. We will not ask you any questions of a personally sensitive nature. Instead, the questions will ask about your familiarity with the video watched. At any time, you may refuse to answer any question or item in any test, inventory, questionnaire, or interview for any reason. The interview will be audio recorded so that we can analyze your responses later. We will keep the recorded files for two months from the date of conducting the interview.

RISKS, STRESS, OR DISCOMFORT

Subjects who participate in this study will be asked demographic questions, questions related technology, and videos they watched. Breach of confidentiality is a risk to being in the study if it happened that your study information, including your audio recording or your responses linked to your name were accidentally given to or were taken by someone who should not have it.

BENEFITS OF THE STUDY

The study is not designed to benefit you. However, the research will generate knowledge which will eventually enable rural communities by futhering the research in low resource environments.

OTHER INFORMATION

Taking part in this interview is voluntary. You can stop at any time. Choosing to take part in this interview, to not take part, or to withdraw from this interview will involve no penalty and will not affect benefits to which you are otherwise entitled. If the findings of the interview are published or presented, we will not use your name. If you think you have a medical problem or illness related to this research, contact Mturk requester who will you for treatment. If you have any questions, please feel free to ask.

Subject’s statement

I confirm by saying "I agree" that this study has been explained to me. I confirm that I volunteer to take part in this research and that I have had a chance to ask questions. If I have questions later about the research, I can the researchers.
