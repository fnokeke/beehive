Welcome to Research on Video Feedback

We are researchers at the University of Washington and Cornell Tech. The goal of this experiment is to collect feedback from users after they watch some videos. Please note that you must know Hindi language in order to participate in the study. You should also have access to headphones and a quiet area to complete the study. Please read carefully and follow all instructions.

Experiment

This experiment will take about 15 minutes to complete. You will be paid $1 at the end of the experiment. You should be able to understand Hindi in order to participate in the experiment.

You will be automatically removed from the experiment with no payment if you don't follow instructions or if you provide fake responses when answering questions.
