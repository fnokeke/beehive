Welcome to Research on Video Feedback

We are researchers at University of Washington and at Cornell Tech. The goal of this experiment is to collect feedback from users after watching some videos.

Please read carefully and follow all instructions.

Experiment

This experiment will take about 15 minutes to complete. You will be paid $1 at the end of the experiment.

You will be automatically removed from the experiment with no payment if you don't follow instructions or if you provide fake responses when answering questions.
