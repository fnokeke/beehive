Welcome to experiment

We are researchers in the Small Data Lab at Cornell Tech, affiliated with Cornell University. This experiment investigates how facebook users use Facebook Mobile App on their smartphone. By participating in this experiment, you will be contributing to the growing body of scientific research on mobile computing and digital consumption.

Compensation

You will receive a payment of $15.00 if you successfully complete the experiment. Payment will be through Amazon gift cards.

Your tasks

This experiment is very simple and straightforward. You tasks are:

  1. Download an android app and use it on your phone for 3 weeks. Setting up the app takes about 3 minutes. You don't have to do anything else throughout the experiment as the app will run quietly in the background so you can go about your normal daily activities.
  2. Complete a survey after setting up the android app. The survey link will appear inside the app.
  3. Complete 2 - 4 surveys that will be emailed to you at the end of the experiment.

How to join

{% include "partials/modals/mturk_consent.html" %} To join the study, you have to read the consent form. Click Here to go through the consent form and select "I agree" if you want to continue. The next set of instructions will appear below after you agree to consent form.

App Setup

  1. On your phone, download firefox browser from playstore (this will help install next app)

  2. Open firefox and enter link to GoodVibe app: http://bit.ly/appgoodv then tap "Open" after app downloads.

  3. GoodVibe Installation page will show up so select “Next” then “Install” on the bottom right then finally “Open” to launch installed app.

  4. If you get error "phone is not compatible" then you cannot participate in experiment because of phone issues. Please uninstall app.

  5. If no error, Tap on “Request Permission” button and toggle “GoodVibe” from “off” to “On”

  6. Then press “back button” on your phone until you return back to “GoodVibe” app.

  7. Next, tap “Start Service” and look at your phone status bar (notification bar) and you should see “Recent usage stats: Facebook 0”

  8. Do this to activate your code:
    1. Open “facebook mobile app” and use it for 15 seconds. A notification will appear telling you to submit your id.
    2. Go back to “GoodVibe” app and enter your email address as "your ID" and tech as "study code".
    3. Submit and your code will appear together with survey link.

      Remember to submit survey. You will not be completely enrolled if you do not complete survey.


  9. App Setup DONE!
    *you can uninstall firefox app now as it was only needed to install GoodVibe app*

Disqualification (with no payment)

You will be automatically removed from the experiment with no payment if:

  • You uninstall app. Uninstalling app means you do not want to participate in experiment anymore.
  • You install app on phone that you don't use regularly.
  • You don't complete surveys or your provide fake response to surveys.
  • Your phone has issues and is not compatible with experiment.

How to know if app is working

Facebook stats will always show in your notification bar throughout the experiment. If it does not show up, open "GoodVibe" app and click "Start Service". If it still doesn't show up then your phone is incompatible with the study.

What does Study App do?

  • The study app will monitor which apps you use on your phone and when you use them. For instance, if you use Facebook at 4pm then the app will record: "Facebook: 4pm". The app will also record when you lock and unlock your phone screen. Please note that the app DOES NOT collect your personal info or any other phone information as our research only focuses on mobile digital consumption.
  • Data collected will automatically be stored in a secured remote server for research analysis at the end of the experiment. This means you don't have to do anything manually.
  • In the second week of the experiment, the app will automatically compute how much time you have used Facebook and then vibrate your phone if you use Facebook past a certain limit. This vibration will not happen in the 1st week and the last week of experiment but only during 2nd - 5th week. Note that vibration is not a bug but it is part of the experiment.
