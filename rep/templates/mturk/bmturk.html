Welcome to experiment

You have been selected from a pool of Mturk workers to participate in this experiment because you successfully followed instructions in a previous HIT. You also indicated that you use Facebook mobile app for at least 5 minutes per day. If you consider this incorrect information, please do not continue with experiment as you will not be paid.

Who we are


We are researchers in the Small Data Lab at Cornell Tech, which is part of Cornell University.

Purpose of this experiment

This experiment investigates how active facebook users use Facebook Mobile App on their smartphone. By participating in this experiment, you will be contributing to the growing body of scientific research on mobile computing and digital distractions.

Duration of experiment

This experiment will run for 3 weeks.

Compensation

You will be paid $15 at the end of the experiment and entered into a lottery draw to receive an additional bonus of $5. Only participants who complete the experiment will be paid.

Your tasks (please read instructions very carefully)

  • Complete three surveys at the beginning of experiment.
  • Download apps that will automatically record which apps you use on your phone. You don't need to do anything manually.
  • Complete two surveys at the end of experiment.
  • That's it. All done!

Disqualification (with no payment)

You will be automatically removed from the experiment with no payment if:

  • you don't complete all surveys at the correct time (3 at the beginning / 2 at the end)
  • you provide fake responses to surveys that you complete.
  • if you don't have experiment apps running throughout the duration of the entire study.

Internet connection required

  • You need to have internet connection on your mobile device throughout the experiment.
  • You need to have Facebook Mobile app already installed on your device.
  • This experiment is only for individuals who actively use facebook mobile app. If you only use Facebook in a mobile browser then you should not continue with experiment.
  • If you meet one of the conditions listed please do not continue with the experiment as you will NOT be paid.

Phone compatibility

If you successfully completed the experiment then your phone will most likely be compatible with our research. However, due to multiple differences in phone vendor softwares, your phone may not work or begin to act weird. If anything odd happens, please discontinue the experiment and contact the requester.

The mobile apps you install have been tested and do not cause any battery drain. However, if you experience serious battery drain then your phone is also not compatible with our experiment. In this case, please contact the requester to exit the experiment.

Throughout the experiment, we will monitor if your phone works and may reach out to you if it doesn't. Please note that if your phone doesn't work, you cannot be paid as it means it is incompatible with the experiment.

How to know if app is working

Facebook stats will always show in your notification bar throughout the experiment. If it does not show up, open "Beevibe" app and click "Start Service". If it still doesn't show up then your phone is incompatible.

What our apps do?

  • The mobile apps you downloaded will monitor all apps you use on your phone throughout the experiment.
  • Data collected will automatically be stored in our remote server for research analysis at the end of the experiment. This means you don't have to do anything manually.

Vibration

In the second week of the experiment, your phone will vibrate if you use Facebook app for more than 10 minutes per day or if you open the app more than 5x per day. This vibration will NOT happen in the 1st week and the 3rd week but only during 2nd week.

Uninstall apps

After the study, you should uninstall all the mobile apps you downloaded for this study as all apps may automatically stop working. To uninstall apps, go to your list of phone apps and uninstall these three apps:

  • Aware
  • Beevibe
