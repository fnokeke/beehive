RescueTime - Participant Onboarding

  1. Create a free account on Rescuetime by clicking here.
  2. Download and install RescueTime on your computer using this link.
  3. Connect RescueTime to Beehive by first logging in here (Google Login).
  4. Then click Connect RescueTime button to connect your account.
  5. Finally, submit your study code provided to you by your researcher.

Congratulations! All Done!
