Mobile App - Participant Onboarding

  1. If applicable, download all non-research apps from Google Play Store.
  2. Install App Logger, an Android research app, on your phone by clicking here.
  3. Install Beehive App, another Android research app, on your phone by clicking here.
  4. Submit your study code provided to you by your researcher to connect to study.
  5. If prompted, allow storage permissions requested by app.
  6. If prompted, enter your weekday/weekend sleep times and wakeup times. You may also need to indicate what time of the day you prefer to receive reminders.
