Consent Form

Participants must be 18-65 years old to participate in this research.

Risks and Benefits

The experiment may interfere with the normal workings of your device. For instance, you may see notification of your facebook usage in the status bar. You may also receive vibration from your phone while using Facebook. However, there are no risks to your device or the apps/software already installed on your device. If you wish to stop participation at any time during the study, please contact the researchers to help you uninstall all softwares and completely unenroll you from the study. The primary risks associated with participation in this experiment is that personal data an be disclosed. Much of this data is already being collected and stored in systems provided by external vendors that were not designed with personal data-sharing policies in mind. We therefore conclude that the additional risks of participating in the study are not substantial. However, we will take great care to protect and secure all project data including using techniques such as encryption, de-identification, etc., as described below. First, we will store the data on secure servers that follow industry-standard practices including cryptography to secure the data. In addition, all communication with these servers will be via encrypted protocols. Second, we will de-identify the data so that accidental release does not immediately identify an individual. With respect to benefits, our system may provide you with deeper insights about your time management habits and how beneficial an intervention could be to you. However, there may also be no personal benefits from your participation in the study. Nonetheless, knowledge received may be of value to humanity.

Taking part is voluntary

Your participation is voluntary. You are free to stop your participation at any point. Refusal to participate or withdrawal of your consent or discontinued participation in the study will not result in any penalty or loss of benefits or rights to which you might otherwise be entitled. The Principal Investigator may at his/her discretion remove you from the study for a number of reasons. In such an event, you will not suffer any penalty or loss of benefits or rights which you might otherwise be entitled. It will not affect your current or future relationship with the institution you were recruited from.

Confidentiality

Your consent form and data will be confidential. Only authorized researchers of the research project will have access to the survey data. Results of the survey will be recorded into a computer and then destroyed. All data will be kept private in password-protected computers. Data communication with installed applications is based on encrypted protocols. When publishing the results, we will not include any information that will make it possible to identify you. Because the recruitment of participants is facilitated by using email and Internet, the message communicated via Internet could be read by a third party due to the nature of Internet technologies. However, the researchers will not intentionally disclose any communication contents to any third parties.
