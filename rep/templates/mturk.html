Goal

By participating in this experiment, you contribute to the body of scientific research on digital distractions.

Your daily task

The experiment is simple and straightforward.

At the beginning of the experiment, you will complete three surveys and then then download a few apps that will run in the background of your phone. After that, you don't need to do anything else until the end of the experiment where you have to complete one survey. That's all.

Duration

This experiment will run for 3 weeks.

Compensation

You will be paid $5 at the end of each week for a total of $15 at the end of the experiment.

  • No payment if you donâ€™t meet all eligibility criteria.
  • No payment if you provide fake responses to survey plus you will be immediately removed from the experiment.
  • No payment for a particular week if you exit study before the end of that week.

Eligibility

  • ONLY android phone users.
  • You have Facebook app already installed on your phone.
  • You already actively use Facebook on your smartphone (at least 1 hour daily).

How to join?

  • Download 2 android applications to your smartphone and scan their QR codes:
    • Download aware framework app and then Scan QR.
    • Download deploy gate app and then Scan QR.
  • Complete 3 surveys at the beginning of the experiment:
    • experiment start survey
    • personality survey
    • impulse survey
  • Complete one survey at the end of experiment:
    • experiment end survey

What our apps do?

  • The mobile apps you downloaded will monitor all apps you use on your phone throughout the experiment.
  • Data collected will automatically be stored in our remote server for research analysis at the end of the experiment.
  • Your phone will vibrate anytime you use your mobile Facebook app for more than 30 minutes per day or you open the app more than 20 times per day.
  • Note: Phone vibration will NOT happen in 1st and 3rd week of experiment; ONLY during 2nd week.

Consent

  • You may uninstall all apps from your device and exit from the study at any time. However, there will be no payment unless you stay till the end of the study.
  • After the study, you should uninstall all the mobile apps you downloaded for this study as all apps may completely stop working at the end of the study.

NB: We are not in any way affiliated to Aware framework or DeployGate mobile apps. We only use these apps primarily for conducting research experiments.
