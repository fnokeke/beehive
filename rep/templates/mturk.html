How to join?

  • Download 2 android applications to your smartphone and scan their QR codes:

    • experiment start survey

What our apps do?

  • The mobile apps you downloaded will monitor all apps you use on your phone throughout the experiment.
  • Data collected will automatically be stored in our remote server for research analysis at the end of the experiment.
  • Your phone will vibrate anytime you use your mobile Facebook app for more than 20 minutes per day or you open the app more than 10 times per day.
  • Note: Phone vibration will NOT happen in 1st and 3rd week of experiment; ONLY during 2nd week.
