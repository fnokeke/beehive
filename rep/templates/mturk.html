Who are we?


We are researchers in the Small Data Lab at Cornell Tech, which is part of Cornell University.

Goal

By participating in this experiment, you contribute to the growing body of scientific research on digital distractions.

Duration

This experiment will run for 5 days.

Your tasks

  • Complete three surveys at the beginning of experiment.
  • Download three apps that will automatically record which apps you use on your phone. You don't need to do anything manually.
  • Complete two surveys at the end of experiment.
  • That's it. All done!

Compensation

You will be paid $1 at the end of each day for a total of $5 at the end of the experiment.

Disqualification

You will not be paid and will be automatically removed from the experiment if:

  • you donâ€™t meet all eligibility criteria listed in the next section.
  • if you provide fake responses to surveys that you complete.
  • if you don't have experiment apps running throughout the study.

Eligibility

  • ONLY android phone users can participate.
  • You actively use Facebook on your smartphone (at least 1 hour daily).

How to join?

  • Download 2 android applications to your android smartphone:
    • Install 1st app by following Aware app installation instructions.
    • Install 2nd app by following DeployGate app installation instructions.


  • Complete these surveys immediately after installing the apps:
    • experiment start survey
    • personality survey
    • impulse survey


  • Complete these surveys on the last day of the experiment:
    • morningness - eveningness survey
    • experiment end survey

What our apps do?

  • The mobile apps you downloaded will monitor all apps you use on your phone throughout the experiment.
  • Data collected will automatically be stored in our remote server for research analysis at the end of the experiment. This means you don't have to do anything manually.
  • Your phone will vibrate anytime you use your mobile Facebook app for more than 15 minutes per day or you open the app more than 5 times per day.
  • Note: Phone vibration will happen ONLY on 2nd and 3rd day of experiment; there will be no vibration on the 1st, 4th and 5th days.

Consent

  • You may uninstall all apps from your device and exit from the study at any time. However, there will only be payments for the days that you participated.
  • After the study, you should uninstall all the mobile apps you downloaded for this study as all apps may automatically stop working.

NB: We are not in any way affiliated to Aware framework or DeployGate mobile apps. We only use these apps primarily for conducting research experiments.

Uninstall apps

To uninstall apps, go to your list of phone apps and uninstall these three apps:

  • Aware
  • Beehive Monitor
  • Deploy Gate

Contact

If you choose to exit early from the study or have any questions, you may contact us by completing this form.
