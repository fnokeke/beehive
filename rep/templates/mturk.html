Goal

The goal of this experiment is to test an app that measures Facebook usage. This experiment does not store any of your data.

Compensation

You will be paid $1.00 after submitting your generated code. Payment will be made within 48 hours of your submission.

**No need to continue with this experiment if you don't already have Facebook app on your android phone.
If you continue, no code will be automatically generated and you will not be paid.**

Your tasks (3 - 5 minutes)

  1. First, download the app and install on your android phone.
  2. Tap Request Permission button and toggle Beehive Monitor to on position.
  3. Return back to app and tap Start Service.
  4. Open Facebook app and leave it for 30 seconds (you'll see the timer automatically update in the status bar).
  5. Then navigate from Facebook app to your home screen and wait for 10 seconds (do this 3x).
  6. If you successfully did this, then a code will be automatically generated in the status bar. Submit this code on the HIT.
  7. After submitting the code, complete this survey.
  8. Uninstall the app after submiting your generated code and the survey.
  9. That's it. All done!
